Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a local AI model?

A local AI model is a type of artificial intelligence that runs directly on your computer or device, rather than relying on a connection to a remote server (the "cloud"). This has several advantages:

  • Privacy: Your data doesn't leave your device, which is crucial for sensitive information.
  • Security: Reduced risk of data breaches since your data isn't transmitted over the internet.
  • Speed: Local processing is often faster than sending data to a remote server and waiting for a response.
  • Offline Access: You can use the AI model even without an internet connection.
  • Cost Savings: You don't have to pay for cloud computing resources.

What are automated agents?

Automated agents are AI programs that can perform tasks and make decisions with minimal human intervention. Think of them as virtual assistants that can handle specific jobs or workflows. They use AI techniques, like natural language processing and machine learning, to understand information, make decisions, and take actions. They are highly customizable and can be designed to fit a very wide range of applications.
